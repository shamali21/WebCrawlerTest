<p><span style='font-size:18.0pt;color:blue'>Course Description</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none'>Information
Retrieval (IR) principles including indexing and searching document
collections, Web search and advanced topics like search in social networks.<span style='font-size:10.0pt'><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p><u>Some of the topics which will be tentatively presented are</u>:</p>
	<ul>
		<li>Vector Model</li>
		<li>Language Models</li>
		<li>IR evaluation methods</li>
		<li>Web search</li>
		<li>MapReduce</li>
		<li>Social networks search</li>
		<li>Word embeddings</li>
	</ul>
